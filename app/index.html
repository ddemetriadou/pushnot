About Us

Fresh Til’ Friday is your little helper when it comes to reducing your Food Waste!

Hundreds of thousands of tonnes of food is thrown out each year while millions starve. It sits in landfill, releasing methane gas into our atmosphere. Fresh Til’ Friday want this to change. We offer a waste management solution which allows you to track your expiring produce. Your eggs only Fresh til’ Friday? No sweat, we’ll remind you to eat them before they go bad!Our vision is a world with less food poverty, less methane emissions and of course saving our users a bit of well-earned dosh.

Our team comprises of students who know only too well how difficult it is to balance budgets and a busy lifestyle when trying to reduce food waste. This is what inspired us to produce this tool.
